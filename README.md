## Scenario

[Project overview document](https://github.com/satokora/CopilotClaimIntelligence/blob/feedf2aab08d5146401c2da935a319441b91eb0b/PoC%20Copilot%20Claims%20Intelligence%20Agent.pdf)

A claims adjuster receives a new claim and must efficiently determine coverage eligibility, assess financial and compliance risk, and prepare a structured summary for review.

The Enterprise Claims Intelligence Copilot assists by retrieving relevant policy coverage details using RAG, applying rule-based risk scoring, and generating a standardized claim assessment summary.

This solution supports informed decision-making, reduces the risk of overpayment and compliance violations, and improves operational consistency while decreasing manual review time.

## Capabilities
- Policy Coverage Retriaval powered by RAG
  - User provides a claim type and System returns deductible, limits, and exclusions.
- Risk Scoring
  - if a claim is greater than a threshold or within flagged regions, high risk score is returned.
- Claim Summary generated by AI
  - System generates adjuster-ready summary with recommendation/
- Telemetry Logging
  - Log intent, risk score, escalation flag
